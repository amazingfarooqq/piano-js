How to Play "Happy Birthday"

  1. Start with two C notes (press 'a' twice)
  2. Play D (press 's')
  3. Back to C (press 'a')
  4. Play F (press 'f')
  5. Play E (press 'd')
  6. Repeat steps 1-3
  7. Play G (press 'g')
  8. Play F (press 'f')
  9. Play C (press 'a')
  10. Play C an octave higher (press 'k')
  11. Play A (press 'h')
  12. Play F (press 'f')
  13. Play E (press 'd')
  14. Play D (press 's')
  15. Finish with A# (press 'u'), A (press 'h'), F (press 'f'), G (press 'g'), F (press 'f')
