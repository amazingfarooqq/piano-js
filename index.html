How to Play "Jingle Bells"

  1. Play E twice (press 'd' twice)
  2. Play E again twice (press 'd' twice)
  3. Play E once more (press 'd')
  4. Play G (press 'g')
  5. Play C (press 'a')
  6. Play D (press 's')
  7. Play E (press 'd')
  8. Repeat steps 1-7 for the second part of the verse
  9. For the chorus, play G three times (press 'g' three times)
  10. Play G again (press 'g')
  11. Play F# (press 't')
  12. Play E (press 'd')
  13. Play D (press 's')
  14. Play A (press 'h')
  15. Play G (press 'g')
  16. Repeat steps 9-15 to complete the chorus
